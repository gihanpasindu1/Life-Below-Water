1. What is Ocean Conservation?

Ocean conservation refers to the protection and preservation of ecosystems in oceans and seas. It involves strategies to reduce human impact, promote biodiversity, and ensure sustainable use of marine resources.

2. Major Threats to the Ocean

  • Plastic pollution: Millions of tons enter the oceans annually.
  • Overfishing: Reduces fish populations and disrupts food chains.
  • Oil spills: Harm marine habitats and wildlife.
  • Climate change: Leads to coral bleaching and sea-level rise.

3. How You Can Help

Everyone can contribute to ocean conservation through everyday actions:

  • Reduce plastic use and recycle properly
  • Support eco-friendly seafood brands
  • Volunteer for beach clean-ups
  • Spread awareness in schools and social media
