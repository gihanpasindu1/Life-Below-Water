Our Mission

Our oceans are the heart of our planet. Covering over 70% of Earth's surface, they provide food, regulate our climate, and support millions of species — including us.

At Life Below Water, we are committed to raising awareness and taking action to protect our oceans under the framework of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 14.

From tackling plastic pollution to conserving coral reefs and marine biodiversity, our mission is to empower individuals and communities to be stewards of the sea.
